## Featured Project: LNG FRONTIER | Vessel DNA Engine
Developed a comprehensive Decision Support System (DSS) designed for real-time LNG cargo analysis and maritime logistics optimization. The application serves as a "Vessel DNA Engine" that bridges the gap between physical shipping constraints and financial arbitrage opportunities.

- **Cargo Calculator & Net Margin Analysis:** Detailed P&L breakdown for individual voyages, accounting for revenue, gas costs (HH), variable opex, charter rates, and canal fees to determine precise Net Margin.
- **Fleet Comparison & Ranking:** Proprietary ranking engine that compares vessel efficiencies (e.g., Gaslog Glasgow vs. Greenergy Star) based on margin contribution and breakeven points for specific global routes.
- **Sensitivity Matrix:** Dynamic risk assessment tool that simulates margin fluctuations based on JKM/TTF price volatility and Charter rates, allowing for rapid stress-testing of trade ideas.
- **Speed & Boil-Off Optimization:** Advanced module to determine optimal vessel speed to balance fuel consumption against time-to-market, minimizing Boil-Off Gas (BOG) impact.
- **Real-time Market Integration:** Live tracking of global benchmarks including Henry Hub (HH), TTF, JKM, and Brent to ensure high-fidelity arbitrage calculations.
